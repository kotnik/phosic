About

You can use this service if you want to have a video from your favourite MP3 and a single static photo.

For any reason you might want that. Be it artistic, or you want to upload an MP3 to Youtube, Vimeo, or DailyMotion...

Using it is free.

Usage

It's pretty simple:

  • Go on a homepage and upload MP3 file and photo.
  • We will make our robots create your video.
  • You can stay on your page and wait for the video, or we will send you an email with link (we will not keep your email for any other reason).
  • Download your video.
  • Come back to us when you want to create new video!

Note: if you don't pick up your video you should know that we will delete everything we have after one day.

Techonology

This service is powered by Flask, a Python micro-framework. Celery does all the heavy-lifting, using RabbitMQ as a broker. Supervisor takes care that everything is properly running. There are no social media buttons or tracking links or analytics JavaScript spyware.
